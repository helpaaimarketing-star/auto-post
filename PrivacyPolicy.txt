SMMA Bot System — Privacy Policy

This bot processes business lead data from public sources (Google Maps, social media profiles)
for social media marketing outreach purposes.

Data Collected:
- Business names, locations, websites, phone numbers (publicly available)
- Social media profile URLs and activity estimates
- Client deal information entered manually via Discord commands
- Email logs for outreach tracking

Data Storage:
- Airtable (cloud database) for lead and deal records
- Local event logs in the event_log/ directory

Data Sharing:
- Outreach emails are sent directly to business contacts
- No data is sold to third parties

Contact:
Configure your agency contact details in the Discord server settings.
